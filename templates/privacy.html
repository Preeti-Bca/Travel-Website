1. Overview

At STARBLUE, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our travel booking services.

Our Commitment: We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes without your explicit consent.

1.1 Scope

This policy applies to:

  • Our website and mobile applications
  • All booking and customer service interactions
  • Marketing communications
  • Third-party integrations and partnerships

2. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us and information that is automatically collected when you use our services.

Personal Information We Collect:
Account Information
  • Name and contact details
  • Email address and phone number
  • Date of birth
  • Password and security questions
Booking Information
  • Travel preferences
  • Passenger details
  • Payment information
  • Special requests

2.1 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, search queries
  • Location Data: General location based on IP address
  • Cookies: Preferences and session information

2.2 Information from Third Parties

  • Social media login information (if you choose to connect)
  • Payment processor transaction details
  • Travel service provider information

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to provide, maintain, and improve our services, as well as to communicate with you.

Service Delivery
  • Process bookings and payments
  • Send booking confirmations
  • Provide customer support
  • Handle cancellations and refunds
Service Improvement
  • Analyze usage patterns
  • Personalize user experience
  • Develop new features
  • Prevent fraud and abuse
Marketing Communications: We may send you promotional emails about our services, but you can opt out at any time using the unsubscribe link.

3.1 Legal Basis for Processing

  • Contract Performance: To fulfill our booking services
  • Legitimate Interest: To improve services and prevent fraud
  • Consent: For marketing communications and cookies
  • Legal Obligation: To comply with applicable laws

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We share your information only as necessary to provide our services and as described in this policy.

4.1 Service Providers

We share information with trusted partners who help us operate our business:

  • Travel Providers: Airlines, railways, bus operators for booking fulfillment
  • Payment Processors: To process transactions securely
  • Technology Partners: For website hosting and analytics
  • Customer Service: To provide support and assistance

4.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when required by law or to:

  • Comply with legal processes or government requests
  • Protect our rights and property
  • Ensure user safety and security
  • Investigate fraud or abuse
No Sale of Data: We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

5. Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure.

Technical Safeguards
  • 256-bit SSL encryption
  • Secure data centers
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls and monitoring
Operational Safeguards
  • Employee training programs
  • Limited access policies
  • Incident response procedures
  • Regular backup systems

5.1 Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide our services to you
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes and enforce agreements
  • Improve our services

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience and analyze website usage.

Types of Cookies We Use:
Essential Cookies

Required for basic website functionality, including security and accessibility features.

Performance Cookies

Help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting anonymous information.

Functional Cookies

Remember your preferences and provide enhanced, personalized features.

Marketing Cookies

Track your activity to deliver relevant advertisements and measure campaign effectiveness.

6.1 Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality.

7. Your Privacy Rights

You have several rights regarding your personal information, depending on your location and applicable laws.

Access and Control
  • Access your personal data
  • Update or correct information
  • Delete your account
  • Export your data
Communication Preferences
  • Opt out of marketing emails
  • Manage notification settings
  • Control data processing
  • Withdraw consent
How to Exercise Your Rights: Contact us at privacy@starblue.com or use the settings in your account dashboard to manage your privacy preferences.

7.1 Data Portability

You have the right to receive your personal data in a structured, commonly used format and to transmit it to another service provider.

7.2 Right to Object

You can object to the processing of your personal data for direct marketing purposes or when processing is based on legitimate interests.
